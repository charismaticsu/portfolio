About Me

Hi! I’m Suhani, a fashion communication student passionate about visual storytelling, system design, and creative thinking. I explore ideas through styling, film, and user-centered research to create meaningful design experiences.

I believe in designing with empathy and intention — from documenting the Bhopal bike system to crafting powerful characters like the Queen of Hearts in a styling project, or directing a short film like *Fark*.
